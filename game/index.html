Instructions:

Press the corresponding arrow key () as soon as the falling game arrow crosses the matching arrow on the "dance floor". Time it just right and your dancing stamina remains the same, but mistime it and your dancing stamina decreases. Too many missteps and you'll have to hang up your dancing fingers.

Get through the song with the highest stamina and be crowned the dancing guru!

Tips:

  1. The dancing floor boxes flash red(miss) or green(success) depending on if the arrow key is pressed at the correct time.
  2. Your stamina decreases 5 for every incorrect dance move. When your stamina drops below 20, the screen will blink red.
  3. After 5 correct dance moves in a row, the screen will blink green. Your stamina increases 10 for every 10 correct dance moves in a row.
